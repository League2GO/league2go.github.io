title: "New feature! - Scorekeepers"
lang: en
localization: scorekeepers
---
You can now add scorekeepers to your league that will enter the information required to populate your statistics. Firstly, the scorekeepers must have an account on League2GO. Then, from the Access tab of your league parameters, add the scorekeepers to authorize them to access to your games.
